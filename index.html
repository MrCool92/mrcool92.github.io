My name is Nikola Đurinec. I currently live in Zagreb, Croatia. I have been working with Unity engine for over 10 years now. I'm the co-owner of Binx Games, a small indie game development studio I helped start. Recently, most of my work has been through it, managing and partnering with contractors.

Send me an email if you would like to see my CV, talk business or have another good reason to.

Here are a few highlights of my expertise:

  • C# Excellence - Writing clean, efficient, and maintainable code is a priority
  • Multiplayer Development - Builing solid multiplayer systems that keep gameplay smooth and synced across networks
  • ECS - Leveraging ECS, jobs and burst to create scalable and high-performance systems
  • UI Implementation - Coding UIs that feel smooth, look great, and make interacting with a game effortless
  • Game Optimization - The challenge of squeezing out every bit of performance while keeping the game looking and feeling great is always embraced
  • Editor Tools - Building tools that make life easier for developers and speed up workflows
  • CI/CD Pipelines - Streamlining development with automated builds and deployments, making iteration faster and smoother
